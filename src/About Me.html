What's up? I'm Akinola Akinpelu - a designer, developer and UX Researcher working to advance my career in the digital product development space.

I was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. Growing up in a digitally progressive age, i was always fascinated by how things worked and how they were made. I loved to create as a kid. From Photography, to Art, to Music. I wasn't talented in all these fields but just like Rock lee says,

A dropout will beat a genius through hardwork

I graduated from the University of Lagos with a B.Sc. in Geology and started working as a geologist. But i felt like i wasn't making an impact. I took control by learning Design in 2021 and my love for creation grew because i now had the chance to help people live more fulfiling lives through product design. I quit my old job and switched careers by late 2021.

By 2022, i wanted to do more and started studying web development. I'm currently a Designer and a Front-end Developer studying to become a full stack Developer.

Currently, I'm the Lead Designer and UX Researcher at Riders in Motion. I lead design strategy for our suite of personalization products. I'm also building my Agency called UX_DEV which will be able to take a product through the complete development cycle.

I'm a Christain currently serving at This Green Church. I'm a major football (Arsenal) and basketball (OKC Thunder) fan, and enjoy Swimming, Playing Guitar and Anime.
